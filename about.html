Para esperimentar


This is a project that was developed by me. The aim of the project is to inform and educate readers and visitors to the page about the impact that civil war has on society. In the country where I was born, the civil war lasted 27 years, having devastated society and destroyed lives and separated entire families and resulted in thousands of deaths, I am one of the survivors of this evil and terrible Angolan civil war that started in the 70s and ended in 2002 , having been born in 1985, this means that I was born in the middle of the crossfire, having my dreams destroyed and my future

Today we face scenarios not of civil war, but of aggressive invasion of one country against another, like the war in Ukraine, Iraq, Libya, etc.The reasons for war most of the times are mainly due to the excessive ambition of individuals or countries, which use their economic and military power to humiliate other countries, but in my country the issue was a little different. Because there are 3 liberation movements in Angola that fought against colonialism, which after independence wanted to divide the country into three states, which left the MPLA party, which had Soviet support disgruntled, thus launching waves of attacks in the capital of Angola - Luanda against other parties, such as the FNLA and UNITA, pushing these parties into the woods and forest, thus starting a war that would last 27 years.There is no peace without consensus on crucial and vital points for the well-being of a society or group of men. This project also aims to give the reader or researcher a broad view of the recurrent problems not only in Angola but also in Africa in general, as I noticed that many people from America, Asia and Europe have little knowledge of the real history of Africa. With this history of Angola's civil war, I believe that many will seek to know more about other countries and learn more about their histories and cultures.
